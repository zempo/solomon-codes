    About


    Hello, Tech World...


    I'm Solomon and I code.

    While studying neuroscience, I supported my degree by teaching young children how to code with OpenWorld Learning (OWL). OWL brought applied math, programming, and independence to disadvantaged children in Denver, Colorado. And for everyone in the program, the experience was life-changing.

    As my students matured and overcame challenges, they inspired me. It only took a few years before I realized an uncomfortable truth: a career in neuroscience would not be best for me. So I took a chance on myself and tried a different path.

    This decision led to a 2 year journey of self, group, and mentored study; a few portfolio projects; and a new perspective. Today, I write clean code, have the mindset to identify and solve problems, and continue to learn more about others and their needs.

    When it's time to step away from the computer, you can find me staying active, exploring LA's social scene, illustrating, and writing.
